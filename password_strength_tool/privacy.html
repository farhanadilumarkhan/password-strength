Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

PasswordStrength Online is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any passwords or personal information you enter into our tools.

Data We Do NOT Collect:

  • Passwords entered into our strength checker
  • Generated passwords from our generator tool
  • Personal information or account details
  • Email addresses (unless voluntarily provided via contact form)
  • Usage patterns or behavioral data

Technical Information We May Collect:

  • Basic web analytics (page visits, general location data)
  • Device and browser information for optimization
  • Theme preferences (stored locally in your browser)

How Our Tools Work

All password analysis and generation happens entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. This means:

  • Your passwords never leave your device
  • No data is transmitted to our servers
  • Processing happens locally for maximum privacy
  • No server-side storage or logging of sensitive data

Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal local storage to enhance your experience:

  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
  • Basic analytics cookies for site improvement
  • No tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts for typography (does not track personal data)
  • Analytics services for basic usage statistics
  • CDN services for improved performance

These services have their own privacy policies and do not have access to passwords or sensitive data entered into our tools.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect our service:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Client-side processing eliminates data transmission risks
  • No password databases or user account systems

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us through our contact page.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
